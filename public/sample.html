What exactly is your goal?
It's important to have a clear and specific goal in mind when starting any project or undertaking. Being specific on your goal provides focus, direction, and motivation. Without a specificity, it's easy to get sidetracked and lose sight of what you want to achieve.

Measurable, Achievable, Relevant

How exactly will you measure the success of your goal?
It is important to have a measurable goal so that you can track your progress and see how close you are to achieving it. This helps to keep you motivated and on track, and also allows you to make any necessary adjustments along the way. A measurable goal should have specific, quantifiable targets such as a date, time, or amount. For example, if your goal is to increase sales, you can set a specific target for the amount of increase you want to see. You can then track your progress by regularly measuring your sales numbers and comparing them to your target. This will give you a clear understanding of how close you are to reaching your goal and help you make informed decisions about what you need to do next to achieve it.
